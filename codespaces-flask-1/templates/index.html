Quick Start

  • Move: Arrow keys (desktop) or touch controls (mobile)
  • Weapons: Hold SPACE for auto-fire, hold/release C for charge shot
  • Modes: Press S for auto-play, F to toggle manual auto-fire assist
  • Audio: Use Enable Audio, Mute, Music, and SFX controls in-game
  • Mobile: Touch controls automatically appear
  • Powerups: Rapid fire, shield, spread, multi-shot, score boost
  • Threats: Dive attacks, meteor hazards, and boss fights every 5 rounds
  • Pacing: Slower opening rounds, then progressive difficulty ramp
